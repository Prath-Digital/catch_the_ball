Game Overview

Welcome to Catch the Ball! This fast-paced arcade game challenges your reflexes, strategic thinking, and hand-eye coordination. Your mission is to catch falling balls of different colors and values while managing multiple objects in motion.

Pro Tip: Stay centered and make small, precise movements. Reading ball patterns is key to achieving high scores!
Quick Start: Move your basket left and right to catch falling balls. The faster and more valuable balls appear more frequently as your score increases!

Rules & Objectives

  • Move your basket to catch falling balls before they hit the ground
  • Each ball color has a different speed and point value
  • Missing any ball will end your current game
  • Your score increases based on the balls you catch
  • The game speeds up gradually as your score increases
  • Chain catches of the same color multiply your points!
  • Challenge friends to beat your high score on the leaderboard
Game Over: Your game ends when any ball hits the ground, so stay alert!

Advanced Strategies

Chain Bonus: Catch multiple balls of the same color in succession for bonus points!
Risk vs Reward: Focus on higher-value balls when you're confident, but don't ignore the easier ones
Position Strategy: Stay near the center to minimize travel distance to any falling ball
Master Challenge: Try to catch three black balls in a row for a massive score boost!
